Refund Policy

At Gayatri Tours and Travels, we strive to ensure your complete satisfaction with our cab services. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions regarding cancellations and refunds. Please read this policy carefully before making a booking.

Cancellation Window

We understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair while maintaining service quality.

Cancellation Timeline:

  • More than 24 hours before pickup: Full refund
  • 12-24 hours before pickup: 75% refund
  • 4-12 hours before pickup: 50% refund
  • Less than 4 hours before pickup: No refund

For package tours and long-distance travel, special cancellation terms may apply. Please refer to the specific booking terms provided at the time of reservation.

Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, your cancellation must meet the following criteria:

  • The booking must be cancelled within the specified cancellation window
  • You must provide your booking ID and verification details
  • The booking must not be in a "no-show" status

Please note that special events, peak season bookings, and certain promotional rides may have different cancellation policies. These will be clearly communicated during the booking process.

Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided in the following situations:

  • No-show cases where the customer fails to appear at the pickup location
  • Cancellations made less than 4 hours before the scheduled pickup time
  • Special event or peak season bookings marked as non-refundable
  • Rides that have already begun or been completed
  • Bookings made using special promotional offers or discount vouchers

Additionally, we reserve the right to deny refunds in the following situations:

  • Cancellation requests made after the allowed refund period
  • Incomplete or missing booking details during the refund request
  • Evidence of abuse or fraudulent booking activity
