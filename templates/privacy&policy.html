1. Introduction

Welcome to Gayatri Tours And Travels. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you interact with our website and services.

2. Information Collection

We collect personal and technical details to serve you better. This includes:

  • Name and contact information (for bookings).
  • Payment information (used only for transactions).
  • Travel preferences and booking details.

Data is collected directly from you, through website usage, or from trusted partners.

3. Use of Information

We use your information to:

  • Book and manage your travel plans.
  • Provide support and manage your account.
  • Offer personalized content and recommendations.
  • Improve our services and website.
  • Send updates, offers, and relevant news.
  • Ensure safety and meet legal requirements.

We use your data only for relevant and lawful purposes.

4. Data Protection

We use strong security measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encrypting sensitive information.
  • Regular security checks and testing.
  • Strict access controls for staff.
  • Regular data backups.
  • Staff training on privacy.

Only authorized people can access your data, and they follow strict confidentiality rules.

We have procedures to handle data breaches and will inform you and authorities if required.

5. Cookie Policy

We use cookies to improve your experience and help our website work better.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Needed for basic site functions.
  • Analytical Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences when you return.
  • Targeting Cookies: Make ads and content more relevant to you.

You can control cookies in your browser settings, but disabling some may affect how the site works.

6. User Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights regarding your personal data, such as:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data.
  • Correction: Ask us to fix any mistakes.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data in some cases.
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data.
  • Portability: Get your data or have it sent elsewhere.
  • Objection: Object to certain uses of your data.
  • Automated Decisions: Opt out of decisions made without human involvement.

To exercise these rights, please contact us. We may ask for details to confirm your identity.

We aim to respond within one month, but complex requests might take longer.

7. Third-Party Services

Our website may link to third-party sites and services. Clicking these links may allow third parties to collect or share data about you. We don’t control these sites and are not responsible for their privacy policies.

We may share your information with third-party providers to help operate our services, including cab service providers, only with your permission.

Please review their privacy policies as they handle your data independently.

8. International Transfers

We may transfer your personal information to countries outside your residence, including countries with different data protection laws.

When we do this, we take steps to keep your information safe, such as:

  • Transferring to countries with adequate data protection.
  • Using approved contracts to ensure your data is protected.
  • Applying technical and organizational safeguards.

By using our services, you agree that your data may be transferred and processed internationally.

9. Changes to Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time due to legal, technical, or business reasons. When we do, we will inform you based on how important the changes are.

If required by law, we will ask for your consent to major changes. You can check the “last updated” date at the top of this policy to see the latest version.

Please review this page regularly for updates on how we protect your privacy.
