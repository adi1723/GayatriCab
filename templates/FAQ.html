We offer a range of vehicles to suit different needs: Economy (hatchbacks), Comfort (sedans), Premium (SUVs), and Luxury (premium sedans and SUVs). You can choose between hourly packages or fixed destination packages depending on your travel needs.

Safety is our top priority. All our drivers undergo thorough background checks and regular training. Vehicles are equipped with GPS tracking, and we have a 24/7 emergency support team. We follow strict COVID-19 safety protocols including regular sanitization of vehicles, mandatory masks, and temperature checks for drivers. Our app also includes an SOS button for immediate assistance.

For airport pickups, the driver will wait with a name placard at the designated meeting point. We offer a complimentary waiting time of 30 minutes for domestic flights and 60 minutes for international flights.

You can save your preferred drivers in your account or mention their ID during booking. We try our best to accommodate such requests, especially for regular customers.

We accept multiple payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and cash. For corporate accounts, we also offer monthly invoicing options. All online transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption to ensure your payment information remains safe.

Our fare is calculated based on the distance traveled, time taken, vehicle type, and any additional services requested. For local city rides, we charge a base fare plus per-kilometer and per-minute rates. For outstation trips, we offer package rates based on distance or duration. All applicable taxes, tolls, and parking fees are additional unless specified otherwise.

No, we believe in complete transparency. The fare estimate provided during booking includes all base charges. Additional charges like tolls, parking, interstate taxes (for outstation trips), and waiting charges are clearly communicated. Night charges (10 PM to 6 AM) may apply for certain bookings, which will be specified during the booking process.

For local city rides, advance payment is optional. For outstation trips and airport transfers, we require a 25% advance payment to confirm your booking. Corporate accounts with approved credit terms can opt for post-service payment. For special events or peak season bookings, full advance payment may be required.

Yes, we have a loyalty program called "Gayatri Rewards" where you earn points for every ride. These points can be redeemed for discounts on future bookings. We also offer seasonal promotions, first-time user discounts, and special rates for corporate clients. Sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on our latest offers.

For local city rides, cancellations made more than 1 hour before pickup are free. For cancellations within 1 hour, a nominal fee applies. For outstation trips, free cancellation is available up to 24 hours before the scheduled departure. Cancellations within 24 hours incur a 15% charge, and within 6 hours incur a 25% charge. No-shows are charged at 50% of the booking amount.

You can cancel your booking through our website or app by going to "My Bookings" and selecting the "Cancel" option. Alternatively, you can call our customer service at +91 98765 43210. Please have your booking ID ready for faster processing. Once cancelled, you'll receive a confirmation email and SMS.

Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days after the cancellation is confirmed. The actual time for the amount to reflect in your account depends on your bank or payment provider. Credit card refunds typically take 5-7 business days, while UPI and net banking refunds are usually faster (2-3 business days).

In the rare event that a driver cancels or doesn't show up, we immediately arrange an alternative vehicle with minimal delay. You'll be notified via call, SMS, and app notification. If the delay exceeds 15 minutes, we offer a 10% discount on your current booking. If we're unable to provide service, a full refund is processed automatically within 24 hours.

Yes, you can modify your booking up to 2 hours before the scheduled pickup for local rides and 12 hours for outstation trips.
