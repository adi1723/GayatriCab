1. Introduction

Welcome to Gayatri Tours & Travels. These Terms and Conditions govern the use of our car travel services.

We provide only Aura Car service, suitable for up to 4 passengers. Customers may choose between AC and Non-AC options based on their preference.

By booking our service, you agree to the following terms. If you do not agree with any part of these Terms and Conditions, please refrain from using our service.

These Terms and Conditions apply to all bookings made with Gayatri Tours & Travels, regardless of whether the reservation is made online, by phone, or in person.

2. Booking Requirements

Booking a tour package with Gayatri Tours & Travels is simple and hassle-free. We provide experienced drivers and take care of all travel logistics.

2.1 Driver Eligibility

No driver eligibility or license is required from customers, as all packages include a professional driver provided by us.

2.2 Required Documentation

We do not require any documentation such as a driver's license, ID proof, or address proof. Just provide your basic contact details when booking.

2.3 Reservation Process

Reservations can be made online, by phone, or in person. To confirm your booking, we require:

  • Your name and contact number
  • Preferred travel dates and destination
  • Advance payment of ₹1000 (non-refundable)

Please note: Booking is only confirmed upon receipt of the ₹1000 advance. Full package details and itinerary will be shared after confirmation.

3. Payment Terms

3.1 Package Charges

Gayatri Tours & Travels offers only travel package services (no daily rentals). Pricing is based on distance traveled:

  • AC Car: ₹25 per kilometer
  • Non-AC Car: ₹21 per kilometer
  • Trips above 300 km are eligible for up to 20% discount

3.2 Additional Charges

The following additional charges apply and are not included in the base per-kilometer fare:

  • Toll charges
  • Parking fees
  • Local taxes or entry charges (if applicable)

3.3 Advance Payment

An advance payment of ₹1000 is required to confirm the booking. This is non-refundable and there is no security deposit required.

3.4 Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

PhonePe
Google Pay (GPay)
Credit / Debit Cards
Paytm

Payment should be completed before the start of the journey. Final charges will be calculated based on actual kilometers and any applicable tolls or local charges.

4. Travel Safety & Coverage

4.1 Standard Safety

All cabs provided by Gayatri Tours & Travels are operated by experienced and professional drivers. Vehicles are regularly maintained and comply with all transport safety norms.

  • Drivers possess valid commercial driving licenses
  • Vehicles are insured as per government transport regulations
  • Regular vehicle health and safety checks

4.2 Passenger Responsibility

While we prioritize your safety, passengers are advised to:

  • Fasten seat belts while traveling
  • Avoid distracting the driver
  • Inform us of any health issues that may require special attention

Please note: We do not provide personal travel insurance. Customers are encouraged to arrange their own coverage if needed.

5. Cab Usage Guidelines

5.1 Permitted Use

Our cab services are strictly for travel purposes. Permitted uses include:

  • Outstation or local sightseeing tours
  • Pilgrimage or leisure travel
  • Airport, railway station, or hotel transfers
  • Family or group travel

5.2 Restricted Use

The following uses are not permitted:

  • Using the cab for commercial purposes like ride-sharing or delivery
  • Driving the vehicle yourself (only our drivers operate the cab)
  • Overloading the vehicle beyond seating capacity
  • Transporting illegal goods or hazardous items
  • Rash behavior or harassment of the driver

5.3 Distance Calculation

All packages are billed based on the total kilometers traveled (starting from garage return point). Charges apply as follows:

  • AC Car: ₹25 per kilometer
  • Non-AC Car: ₹21 per kilometer
  • Additional charges: Toll, parking, and local permit fees

Any misuse of the cab service may result in cancellation of the trip without refund and potential blacklisting from future bookings.

6. Cancellation & Modification Policy

6.1 Cancellations

We understand plans may change. Our cancellation charges are as follows:

  • 48+ hours before scheduled pickup: No cancellation charge
  • 24–48 hours before pickup: 25% of the total booking amount
  • Less than 24 hours before pickup: 50% of the total booking amount
  • No-shows: 100% of the booking amount will be charged

6.2 Modifications

Changes to your booking can be made up to 24 hours before the scheduled trip time, subject to availability:

  • Date or time changes: Free if requested at least 24 hours in advance
  • Destination changes: Charges may apply based on additional kilometers
  • Pickup or drop-off point updates: Subject to route adjustment and fare recalculation

All cancellations and modifications must be made through our official contact number or website. Refunds (if applicable) are processed within 5-7 business days.
