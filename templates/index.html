Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, booking process, and policies.

You can book a cab through our website, mobile app, or by calling our customer service number. Simply enter your pickup location, destination, date, time, and select the type of cab you need. You'll receive a confirmation once your booking is confirmed.

Cancellations made more than 24 hours before the scheduled pickup time are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations made between 12-24 hours before pickup incur a 25% charge. Cancellations made less than 12 hours before pickup incur a 50% charge. No-shows are charged the full amount.

No, tolls, parking charges, and other local charges are not included in the base fare. These will be charged separately based on actual expenses incurred during the journey. You will receive a detailed breakdown of all charges in your invoice.

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), and cash. For online bookings, we require a 25% advance payment to confirm your reservation.

Yes, we offer multi-day packages for outstation trips. You can book a cab for multiple days, and we'll provide you with a custom quote based on your itinerary. For multi-day bookings, driver allowance charges will apply in addition to the base fare.
