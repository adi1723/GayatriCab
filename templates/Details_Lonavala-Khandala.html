Package Overview

Experience the serene hill station of Lonavala, famous for its lush greenery, waterfalls, and pleasant weather. This 2-day package lets you explore beautiful valleys, enjoy scenic viewpoints, and visit popular attractions like Bhushi Dam and Tiger’s Leap. Perfect for a quick weekend getaway from Pune or Mumbai.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1

Pune to Lonavala & Khandala Sightseeing

7:00 AM

Pickup from Pune

Our driver will pick you up from your location in Pune in a comfortable Aura car.

9:30 AM

Arrive at Lonavala

Start your day with a visit to the famous Karla and Bhaja Caves, ancient rock-cut Buddhist caves.

12:00 PM

Lunch at Local Restaurant

Enjoy authentic Maharashtrian cuisine at a popular local restaurant.

1:30 PM

Visit to Tiger’s Leap and Lion’s Point

Explore scenic viewpoints offering breathtaking valley views and waterfalls.

4:30 PM

Check-in at Hotel

Check-in to your hotel in Lonavala/Khandala, relax and freshen up.

7:00 PM

Visit Lonavala Market

Explore local markets for famous chikki, fudge, and souvenirs.

8:00 PM

Dinner & Overnight Stay

Return to hotel for dinner and overnight rest.

Day 2

Khandala & Return to Pune

8:00 AM

Breakfast at Hotel

Enjoy a relaxed breakfast at the hotel.

9:30 AM

Visit Rajmachi Fort

Explore the scenic Rajmachi Fort and its surroundings (optional short trek).

12:30 PM

Visit Lonavala Lake

Relax by the serene Lonavala Lake and enjoy the peaceful atmosphere.

1:30 PM

Lunch

Lunch at a local restaurant in Khandala.

2:30 PM

Departure for Pune

Begin the journey back to Pune with scenic views of the Western Ghats.

4:30 PM

Drop-off in Pune

Arrive at your location in Pune, concluding the trip.
