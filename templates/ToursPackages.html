Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our tour packages

A minimum of 3 passengers is required to book any of our tour packages. This is because our packages are designed for the Aura car, which can accommodate 3-4 passengers comfortably. The pricing is calculated on a per-person basis with a minimum of 3 passengers.

Our package prices include transportation in an Aura car, accommodation in a standard hotel room, all meals (breakfast, lunch, and dinner), and sightseeing as mentioned in the itinerary. Entry fees to certain attractions, personal expenses, and any activities not mentioned in the itinerary are not included.

Yes, we offer customization options for our packages. You can request changes to the itinerary, hotel upgrades, or additional activities. Please note that customizations may affect the package price. Contact our customer service team to discuss your specific requirements.

We require a 50% advance payment at the time of booking to confirm your reservation. The remaining balance must be paid at least 3 days before the journey date. We accept payments through various methods including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and digital wallets.

We offer pick-up and drop-off services from any location within Pune city limits. The exact pick-up time will be communicated to you a day before the journey. For locations outside Pune city limits, additional charges may apply.
